Lisa Burton General manager
My role is to manage the operational side of the Union including management of the staff team whilst developing the Union with the Trustee Board, students and staff colleagues. I work closely with the Elected Officers providing support, training and advice and work with the Board on creating our strategy, Charity policies and procedures. I am the senior staff member within the Union. I am passionate about the positive impact Student Unions’ play in people’s lives. Students or partners might want to contact me if they have any questions about the charity or wish to support our charitable aims.
